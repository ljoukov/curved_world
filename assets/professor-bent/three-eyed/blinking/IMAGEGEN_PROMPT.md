strict identity, anatomy, costume, pose, scale, camera, and style reference.

```text
Create exactly six consecutive eye-blinking frames in a precise 3-column by
2-row grid. Change only the eyelids; keep the mouth in the same neutral smile.
Lock the head, nose, mouth, eyebrows, three spectacle frames, hair, shoulders,
costume, lighting, camera, and crop. All three eyes blink together.

Frame order: fully open; 25 percent closed; 65 percent closed; fully closed;
65 percent closed while reopening; 25 percent closed while reopening. In the
fully closed frame, preserve three distinct closed-eye locations inside all
three gold lenses.

Use a perfectly flat uniform #ff00ff chroma-key background with no shadows,
gradients, texture, floor, halo, vignette, reflections, scenery, labels,
borders, or dividers. Do not use #ff00ff on the character. No hands, props,
text, logos, or watermark. Preserve the reference's polished painterly 2D
raster character-render style.
```
